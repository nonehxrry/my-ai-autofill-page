Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use Autofill for job applications? +

Using Autofill can help you in two major ways:

  1. It saves you significant time. Autofill uses your profile and tailored resume to automatically complete all job application fields. You no longer need to spend hours manually entering the same information again and again.
  2. You can apply to jobs in seconds, giving you more chances to land interviews and increasing your odds of getting hired.
Can I use Autofill with every job application site? +

Our AI is designed to work with most major Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and job boards. While we strive for universal compatibility, some niche sites might have unique structures. We are constantly updating and expanding our support.

How does Job Application Autofill work? +

Once you install our browser extension and set up your profile, simply navigate to a job application form. Our AI scans the form fields, matches them with your profile data, and fills them in instantly. You'll then review and submit!
